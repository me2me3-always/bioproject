Introduction
Women empowerment and healthcare are closely linked. In the context of breast and cervical cancer, empowering women socially and economically is vital for timely diagnosis and effective treatment.

Cancer and Its Impact on Women
Breast and cervical cancers are leading threats to women. Due to stigma, lack of awareness, and healthcare access, many women face late diagnoses. Cultural norms often prevent timely medical help.
How Women Empowerment Enhances Cancer Treatment
Increased Awareness and Education: Leads to early detection and better outcomes.
Improved Access to Healthcare: Financial independence enables timely treatment.
Reduction of Stigma: Open dialogue reduces fear and encourages early help-seeking.
